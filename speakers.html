Speakers - TEDxMEC 2019

Sachin Rajeev

When four friends decide to get together to make music, they've got to make sure that they're one hell of a band. And that's what they are. Listen to the lead vocalist of Chidakasha, Sachin Rajeev speak on their venture through bold and daring tunes in the world of music.

Dr. Arun A. Shanmugham

When you look at a bear, you see a beast. A terrifying creature that will potentially rip you to shreds. But when renowned bear rescue activist Dr. Arun A Shanmugham looks at a bear, he sees his own emotions reflected back at him. His own fears become irrelevant in his pursuit to provide these innocent souls a home. Come listen as he spells out his efforts and encounters alongside @wildlifesos in protecting the bare necessities of these beautiful animals, at TEDxMEC 2019.

Jegatha Muralidharan

Have spotlights ever put you on the spot? Ever been scared and nervous, not wanting to be the center of attention? Here's a dancers perspective on how to embrace the performer within you, and to be less afraid in life. Learn how to kick ass at TEDxMEC 2019!

Dr. Sarita Vig

How does one picture the unseen? Black holes are cosmic entities that have captured the imagination - and fear - of humans for aeons. Listen as Prof. Sarita Vig, Astrophysicist & Associate Professor at IIST, sheds light on the hidden secrets and astonishing mysteries of these extreme cosmic objects and how they warp spacetime, only at TEDxMEC.

Moosa Mehar

Engineers once were considered this and much more. Why is it then, that the words "I'm an engineer" now provokes contempt? Listen to Moosa Meher, Founder of TinkerHub, explain this shift in perspective and ways to tackle it, only at TEDxMEC.

Vishnu Soman

Unbroken. Unafraid. Unbeaten. These words do not comprise the conventional tone used to describe people with disabilities. But as Vishnu reveals, the state of your body is not what defines you. It's what's inside. Join us in this incredible journey of courage and kindness, and a whole package of miracles. After all, disability is just a matter of perspective.

Gitanjali Natarajan

Fear. Exhaustion. Anger. A thousand emotions flit through our minds a day, yet we don't stop to listen. Take a moment at TEDxMEC, with Dr. Geetanjali Natarajan as she shares her perspective on the mystery of human psychology, and ways to tackle the beasts within.
